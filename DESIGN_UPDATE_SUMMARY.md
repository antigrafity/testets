## 💡 Why Cyan?

✨ **Modern & Tech-Forward**
- Perfect for innovative company profiles
- Highly visible on dark backgrounds
- Premium and professional aesthetic

🎨 **Visual Impact**
- Strong contrast with dark theme
- Sophisticated and elegant
- Stands out without being aggressive

🔧 **Technical Benefits**
- Excellent accessibility (high contrast ratio)
- Works well in all lighting conditions
- Versatile for various UI elements

## 📝 Color Usage Guide

### Use Cyan (`#00D9FF`) For:
- ✅ Logo and branding
- ✅ Secondary action buttons
- ✅ Brand highlights and accents
- ✅ Premium UI signals
- ✅ Hover states on secondary elements
- ✅ Active states on navigation

### Keep Blue (`#1447E6`) For:
- ✅ Primary action buttons
- ✅ Primary CTAs
- ✅ Links
- ✅ Active states on primary elements

### Keep Other Colors For:
- ✅ Success: Teal (`#009588`)
- ✅ Error: Red (`#E40014`)
- ✅ Warning: Gold (`#FCBB00`)
